The Process Items and Brands We Buy

We’re looking for surplus or used industrial automation parts, controls, and electronics. Below is a general list of what we’re most interested in — but if it’s industrial and you’re not sure, reach out anyway!

We buy New, New Old Stock (NOS), and Used items. Whether your parts are still sealed in the box or pulled from a working machine, we’d love to take a look.

Common Categories

  • PLC systems (controllers, I/O modules, base units)
  • Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)
  • Servo drives and motors
  • HMI / touchscreens
  • Power supplies and transformers
  • Industrial PCs and monitors
  • Sensors and switches
  • Safety relays, contactors, timers
