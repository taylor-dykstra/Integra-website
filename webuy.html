The Process Items and Brands We Buy

We purchase surplus and used industrial controls, automation components, and electronics — whether you’re upgrading equipment, closing a facility, or simply clearing out unused parts.

We’re based in West Michigan but open to deals across the U.S. Reach out to see how we can help turn your unused inventory into cash.

How It Works

  • Send Us What You’ve Got
    Email us a list, spreadsheet, or photos of your surplus inventory. No need to organize — we’ll sort through it.
  • Fast Review & Quote
    We typically respond within 1 business day with an offer. For larger lots, we can arrange a call or site visit.
  • We Arrange Pickup or Shipping
    We cover shipping costs. If you're in West Michigan, we’ll even pick it up ourselves.
  • You Get Paid Fast
    Once the items are received and verified, we send payment via your preferred method — ACH, check, PayPal, etc.
